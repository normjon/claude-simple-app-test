User App has been deployed!

To verify the deployment:

1. Check pod status:
   kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "user-app.name" . }}"

2. Check pod distribution across AZs (for Karpenter verification):
   kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "user-app.name" . }}" -o wide

3. Test the health endpoint:
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "user-app.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }}
   curl http://localhost:8080/health

4. View logs:
   kubectl logs -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "user-app.name" . }}" -f
